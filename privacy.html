privacy policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how About Nine (the "App") collects, uses, analyzes, discloses, and protects your personal information. Please read this policy carefully before using the App.

The App is provided by Dalbit Arae Eoheung, an individual business operator organized under the laws of the Republic of Korea ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), and is offered to users located in the United States and Canada on a cross-border basis.

Cross-Border Notice: Your personal data, including voice data, will be transferred to and processed in the Republic of Korea. Data protection laws in Korea may differ from those in your country or province of residence. By using the App, you acknowledge and consent to this international transfer.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account registration details (e.g., name, date of birth, email)
  • Profile information and dating preferences
  • User-generated content, including voice recordings and prompts
  • Communications with our customer support team

1.2 Voice and Conversation Data (Biometric Information)

As a voice-based dating service, we collect voice data generated during in-app conversations. This may include audio recordings, voice feature extracts, timestamps, interaction context, and related metadata.

Depending on your jurisdiction, voice data may constitute biometric information under applicable law, including the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act (BIPA), Texas CUBI, Washington My Health MY Data Act, and similar statutes.

  • Purpose: Provide the service, improve matching, and maintain safety and moderation systems.
  • No Biometric Authentication: We do not use voice data for security, authentication, or identification unless required by law or with separate consent.
  • No Sale of Biometric Data: We do not sell, lease, trade, or profit from your voice or biometric data.
  • Retention: Voice data is retained only as long as necessary and deleted within 90 days following account deletion unless otherwise required by law.

By using voice features in the App, you expressly consent to the collection, storage, and processing of your voice data as described in this Policy.

1.3 User Behavior and Interaction Data

We collect information about how you interact with the App, including:

  • Matching, swiping, skipping, and connection activity
  • Conversation frequency, duration, and response patterns
  • Feature usage, navigation paths, and in-app actions
  • Error logs, crash reports, and performance data

This behavioral data helps improve matching accuracy, user experience, and safety systems.

1.4 Automatically Collected Technical Information

  • Device type, operating system version, and app version
  • IP address and general location (country or region level)
  • Time zone, language settings, and network type
  • Session duration and crash logs

1.5 Precise Geolocation Data

With your explicit permission, we may collect precise geolocation to enable distance-based matching. You can withdraw permission via device settings, though certain features may be limited.

1.6 Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies, mobile SDKs, and pixels to:

  • Remember preferences and session state
  • Analyze usage trends and engagement
  • Measure feature performance and reliability
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security

You may control cookies via browser or device settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect functionality. We do not use cookies for cross-context behavioral advertising.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App
  • Enable voice-based communication between matched users
  • Analyze behavior to improve usability and engagement
  • Train, evaluate, and improve matching algorithms (often aggregated or de-identified)
  • Personalize content and match suggestions
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or safety risks
  • Monitor performance, troubleshoot issues, and debug errors
  • Respond to inquiries and provide support
  • Send service-related notifications
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We will not use your data for purposes materially inconsistent with the above without your consent.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

  • Consent: Voice data, biometrics, geolocation, and processing beyond what is necessary to provide the service.
  • Contract Performance: Delivering the App and requested services.
  • Legitimate Interests: Fraud prevention, security, service improvement, and analytics when not overridden by your rights.
  • Legal Obligations: Compliance with applicable laws.

For Canadian users, consent is the primary legal basis under PIPEDA and provincial laws.

4. Machine Learning and Automated Processing

We use machine learning to analyze profiles, preferences, behavioral signals, and voice data to generate match recommendations and improve service quality. Automated systems support—rather than replace—human judgment and do not make legal or similarly significant decisions without human review.

Quebec users: Under Law 25, you may challenge decisions made using automated processing by contacting support@aboutnine.com.

5. Information Shared with Other Users

To facilitate matching, other users may see or hear:

  • Profile details, preferences, and photos you share
  • Voice recordings or prompts created for your profile
  • Live voice data during conversations with matches

You are responsible for what you share. Exercise caution, and remember the App does not conduct background checks on users.

6. How We Share Information with Third Parties

We do not sell personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Cloud, voice, analytics, security, and support vendors bound to protect your data.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect user/public safety.
  • Business Transfers: If involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, with notice and safeguards.
  • With Your Consent: For any other purpose.

7. International Data Transfers

The Company operates from Korea. Your personal data may be processed there and in other jurisdictions where service providers operate.

Safeguards: We implement technical, organizational, and contractual safeguards, including data processing agreements.

Quebec — Law 25: A Privacy Impact Assessment is conducted before transferring Quebec users' data abroad. Contact us at support@aboutnine.com for details.

8. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while the account is active; deleted or anonymized within 30 days after deletion unless required for legal, safety, or fraud prevention purposes.
  • Voice Data: Deleted within 90 days after account deletion unless law requires longer retention.
  • Behavioral/Analytics Data: Retained in aggregated or de-identified form for up to 2 years for product improvement.
  • Legal Holds: Certain data may be retained longer as required by law or ongoing proceedings.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 United States — California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents may:

  • Request to know categories/specific pieces of personal data
  • Request deletion (subject to exceptions)
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Confirm we do not sell/share personal data
  • Limit use of sensitive data (voice, geolocation)
  • Exercise rights without discrimination

Email support@aboutnine.com with "California Privacy Request." We verify identity and respond within 45 days (extendable by 45 days).

9.2 United States — Other States

Residents of Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut, Texas, and other states with privacy laws may access, correct, delete, or opt out of certain processing by emailing support@aboutnine.com with "State Privacy Request."

9.3 Canada — PIPEDA and Provincial Laws

  • Right of Access
  • Right to Correction
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (subject to restrictions)
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint with the OPC or provincial authorities

9.4 Quebec — Law 25

  • Right to Data Portability
  • Right to human review of automated decisions
  • Right to de-indexing where technically feasible

Contact support@aboutnine.com with "Quebec Privacy Request" to exercise these rights.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If we learn of such collection without parental consent, we delete the data. Contact support@aboutnine.com if you believe a minor has provided data.

11. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including encryption in transit/at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is completely secure; in the event of a breach posing real risk of significant harm, we will notify affected users and authorities as required.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Policy. Material changes are announced in-app or by email at least 14 days before taking effect (or as required by law). Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
